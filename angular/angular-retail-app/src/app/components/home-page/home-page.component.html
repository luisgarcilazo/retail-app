Retail Management App

Create an account, access the store, and add products to cart to then checkout and create an order.

Create an account

Create an account to be able to place orders and keep track of all your orders and their status.

Check the store and add items to cart

An ample selection of items, divided into 6 different categories in the store page, with a display all tab to see all the items. Amazing prices for the average person!

Check out in the cart

After you have added your items to cart and logged in, access the cart page to start checking out. You are able to add a promo code during check-out and apply it for a discount.
